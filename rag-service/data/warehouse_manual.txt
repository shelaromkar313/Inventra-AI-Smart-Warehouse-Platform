SMART WAREHOUSE SAFETY PROTOCOLS - 2024
--------------------------------------

1. FORKLIFT OPERATIONS (ZONE A)
- Maximum speed in Zone A is 5 km/h.
- Operators must sound horn at every intersection.
- Battery charging is only permitted in the designated area near Dock 2.

2. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS (ZONE B)
- All chemical spills must be reported to the Floor Manager immediately.
- Use the orange spill kits located next to the hazard signage.
- Full PPE (Protective Personal Equipment) is mandatory in Zone B.

3. INVENTORY Tarrivals must be scanned using the CV Platform at the entry gate.
- Low stockRACKING (SYSTEM-WIDE)
- All new  alerts are triggered when quantity falls below 10 units.
- ML forecasting runs every 24 hours to optimize restock cycles.

4. FIRE SAFETY
- Fire extinguishers are color-coded. Red for CO2, Blue for Powder.
- Assembly point for all staff is the East Parking Lot.
